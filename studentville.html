Student Life

Dunedin is well known for the student life, from the parties to the great education at Otago University. Dunedin has a great student vibe which brings in many new students each year who want to experience the greatness of being a Dunedin student.

Dunedin offers many different experiences for their students which help them learn and get inspired to become successful in the future. Otago University offers many courses too Dunedin students, these range from arts and science to health.


#Want to change out for a paralax sometime

The Otago University campus. The Otago University campus is designed to allow students to take their learning outside and enjoy the nature that the amazing campus offers while at the same time having a comfy space to get their studies done.

Dunedin Students are known to be party freaks. The notorious Hyde Street party is known as Dunedin's greatest street party with hundreds of students who attend. This party brings the students together to celebrate their success and just have a great time.
